Delicious meatpie recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g bugger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

      1. Boil the meat until al dente.
      2. heat oliive oil in a pan and saute garlic.
      3. Add tomato sauce aand simmer for 5 minutes.
      4. Mix the bugger with the sauce and season to taste.
      5. serve hot and enjoy!
